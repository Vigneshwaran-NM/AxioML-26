About AxioML'26

AxioML'26 is an intensive 24-hour offline hackathon organized by the Department of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning at Prathyusha Engineering College. This event brings together innovative minds to tackle cutting-edge challenges across AI, AR/VR, ML Resilience, EdTech, and Healthcare.

Join fellow innovators for a day of intense coding, learning, and collaboration. Build groundbreaking projects, compete for exciting prizes, and network with industry mentors. Whether you're exploring AI algorithms or creating immersive AR experiences, AxioML'26 is your platform to shine.
